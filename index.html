Projects

Veganism Campaign @ HKVegSoc

Working with a core team of animal activists to promote veganism among general public via social media, street events and demonstrations, aiming to lower the suffering of animals.

Food Uncovered

Revealing the hidden truth of global/local food market as well as the animal food production industry by researching and publishing articles, enabling the public to make an "informed choice".

HK-EU e-Learning Collaborative Project

Project coordinator of the e-Learning Project for more than 100 Hong Kong / European secondary school students to learn about green living and sustainability in their community on Plastic, Food, City and Fashion. Work involved content creation, platform management as well as liasion with teachers and students.

Exhibtion of "Hamburg - A European Green Capital"

Editing (text & layout) and coordination of an 30-panel exhibition "Green, Inclusive Growing City by the Water: Perspectives on Urban Development", showcasing the masterplan of urban development of Hamburg.

AIHK Human Rights Documentary Film Festival

Translation and editing of subtitles from English to Chinese for over 8 films including "In Prison My Whole Life" & "Malala: A Girl from Paradise" from 2013 onwards.

Cities for Life Day

Annual street event held by Amnesty International HK from 2012 onwards. I was in charge of the open air installation work which allows the public to feel the fear of the inmates being in death row, and re-building a connection to the subject.
